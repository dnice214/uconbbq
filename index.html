What's New This Year

Every year we make it better. Here's what's coming to the 2026 BBQ.

Why you'll be there

California's Contractors, Together Since 1980

What began as a small gathering has grown into the industry's signature summer event — the one place where general contractors, subcontractors, and the people who keep California building all show up in the same place, at the same fire.

Award-winning BBQ & chili

Legendary barbecue, 16 teams competing for the Chili Cook-Off trophy, and a tasting vote that's yours to cast. This is what we come for.

Live music & camp games

Live entertainment, lawn games, and good conversation all evening long — the full camp experience, no agenda required.

Your whole industry

~1,200 contractors, tradespeople, and industry partners in the same place. The connections you make here last all year.

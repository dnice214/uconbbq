2026 additions

New at Camp This Year

The UCON Tent

Once you're checked in, swing by the UCON Tent to grab some UCON swag and meet the team behind the event. A relaxed spot to say hello and pick up something to take home.

Food Stations

No buffet line this year. Food stations replace the traditional buffet, so you grab what you want, when you want it. More options, less waiting.

Camp-Style Games

Cornhole, lawn games, and more set up across the grounds. Great way to kill time between chili tastings or just take a break from networking.

Collector's item

The 2026 Limited-Edition Pin

Limited edition

Take home a piece of Camp UCON. This year's limited-edition 2026 BBQ & Chili Cook-Off pin will be available to attendees at the event while supplies last — collect yours before they're gone.

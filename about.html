From Campfire to Cook-Off

About the BBQ

You're invited to UCON's most cherished and widely attended event of the year.

The gathering

Over 40 Years Strong

Thursday, August 6. Walnut Grove at the Alameda County Fairgrounds, Gate 8, Pleasanton. The 2026 UCON BBQ brings together over 1,200 people from across Northern California's construction industry, for a summer evening of real BBQ, a serious chili competition, live music, and the kind of industry networking that actually happens when you take the ties off.

It started in 1980 with a handful of contractors and has been running every summer since. The industry has changed a lot in over 40 years. The BBQ hasn't. That's exactly the point. If you've been before, you already know. If you haven't, 2026 is the year.

Event leadership

Event Chair

Will Murphy

Event Chair
Argent Materials, Inc.

Will has spent six years at Argent Materials, a leading concrete and asphalt recycling operation and aggregate supplier in Northern California. Working his way from Sales Representative to Sales Lead, he built strong relationships across the construction sector and specializes in sustainable, cost-effective solutions for contractors. A longtime UCON Associate Committee member for two years, Will brings that same hands-on commitment to his role as 2026 Event Chair.

On the day

Camp Rules

Taste every entry

Tasting opens at 5:00 PM. Walk the row, grab samples from all 15 booths, and form your opinions fast. Voting closes at 5:45.

Two ways to win

A panel of judges scores each chili on taste, heat, and presentation. Meanwhile, you vote for your People's Choice favorite. Two trophies, two very different criteria. The judges don't always agree with the crowd.

Awards at 6:15

Judges' Choice, People's Choice, and Best Booth are all announced at 6:15 PM. The losing teams start planning their 2027 comeback immediately.

For competing teams

The Details

Team information

There is no additional fee to enter the Chili Cook-Off. All attendees planning to work at the Chili Cook-Off booth must register and pay for a BBQ ticket. Competing teams must sign and return the Chili Cook-Off Rules & Regulations Commitment form. Questions? Email Rachel Oraa at roraa@unitedcontractors.org.
